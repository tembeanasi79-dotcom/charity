About Nizza Foundation

A community-driven non-profit organization committed to transforming lives through education, conservation, health, and sustainable development across Kenya.

Support Our Mission

Our Story

Nizza Foundation was established with a belief that sustainable change begins within communities themselves.

Across Kenya, communities face barriers in education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and economic opportunity. We exist to address these challenges through practical, long-term solutions.

What began as small initiatives has grown into a structured foundation focused on measurable and lasting impact.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Empower underserved communities through education, conservation, health, and sustainable livelihood programs.

Vision: A Kenya where every community has equal access to opportunity and sustainable development.

What We Do

Education

Scholarships, mentorship, and school support for learners.

Conservation

Tree planting and environmental protection initiatives.

Health & Wellness

Community outreach and health awareness programs.

Community Development

Skills training and entrepreneurship support.

How We Work

1. Community Engagement

Understanding real needs through local collaboration.

2. Program Design

Developing tailored solutions for each community.

3. Implementation

Executing programs with community participation.

4. Impact Evaluation

Measuring outcomes and improving continuously.

Our Impact

Impact is measured not only in numbers but in transformed lives and stronger communities.

From education to livelihoods and environmental restoration, our work focuses on lasting change.
